A Virtual Assistant can support you with a wide range of business administration tasks, including calendar management, inbox management, invoicing, data entry, research, customer support, and social media scheduling. Furthermore, I also offer technical support, project management, web development, website maintenance, and web hosting services. Whatever your requirements, I provide flexible support to keep your business running smoothly.

Rates are available [here]. Ad hoc services are billed after the fact with a minimum of 1 hour required, and thereafter in 15-minute increments. Retainer packages are tracked in 6-minute blocks and remain valid for one calendar month, with the option to roll over unused time for up to one additional month. Retainer payments are made in advance to ensure seamless service.

I understand that unexpected situations can arise, and I handle these with flexibility and empathy to accommodate your needs as best as possible.

I take data protection and confidentiality seriously.
