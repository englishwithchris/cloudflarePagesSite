Join MothMan at the West Virginia Folklore Festival

Join us this July to welcome the opening of the West Virginia Folklore festival, running from July 25 to August 1. We will be playing a selection of our greatest hits including 'Whenceforth The Mothman' and 'A Wendigo in Paris (Tennesse)'. Help us support our local opening acts and join us for an evening of bluegrass roots, local IPAs, and some great American bar food at Earl's Speakeasy and Grill. Space is limited and online tickets are available now.
